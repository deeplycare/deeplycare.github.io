    About Us

    Deeply Care is a multi-service charitable non-profit organisation that assist the entire newcomer family in achieving settlement and integration within the Grater Toronto Area and beyond.

    "Community That Cares"

    Deeply Care was established in year 202 by like minded men and women philanthropists and community enhancers who belive in common goof and assume the role of community change agents. Deeply case is one of the social services agencies in Toronto that plans to delivering integrated programs from physical, mental health and disability services to affordable housing pathways to employment. We have worked with communities to improve health and well being for uniquely abled children, seniors, single mothers, newcomers and youth creating new opportuinites to thrive. Deeply Care has been operational in many communities in GTA area since 202 however we are looking forward to working with not only the communities in GTA but all over Ontario and Canada.

    We strive for equal access to opportunites, enabling immigrants to connect to the enduring values and principles of what defines Canada. Deeply Care hopes to foster loyalty towards Canada from our new immigrants and their families.


    We encourage this by providing a variety of holistic services in a friendly and open atmoshphere. Our vision is to continue to evolve together as a community!

    Deeply Care plans partners with health and social agencies in GTA to deliver integrated care to reduce poverty and promote community health and well being. We plan to work with seniors, adults, youth and families to provide safe and affordable education options, diabaility support and a wide range of physical and mental health services. We also plan to offer newcomer settlement programs, support for single mothers, child care, financial services and pathways to meaningful employment.

    The main objectives of the organisation:

    To imporve the lives if poor people and new immigrants in Ontario. Assist low income families, personal home care, home support, medical alert system, meals on wheels.

    • 01 Holistic Development

      Holistic devlopment of the community with emphasis on imporving quality of life and build better and stronger communities.

    • 02 Quality of Education

      Improving quality of education for children in low income communities.

    • 03 Poverty and Diversity

      Sensitizing community on the issues of poverty, diversity, racism and race relations.

    • 04 Entrepreneurship

      Oragnizing training capacity, building and promoting entrepreneurship.
